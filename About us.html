WHO WE ARE

The F2 are the worlds largest social media influencers in football and Europes Largest in Sport. Billy Wingrove and Jeremy Lynch have successfully built a social media following of over 30m across all platforms and have worked with some of the Worlds largest companies.




The F2 Freestylers YouTube channel was launched in 2011 bringing insane football trickshots and incredible “Tekkers” to a global audience. Fast Forward to 2020 and they have amassed over 1bn views across over 70 countries and have firmly cemented themselves as the leading football influencers in the World working daily alongside sporting stars and legends. >
